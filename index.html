Esteban Gallardo

Computer Engineer & Multimedia Developer

I'm Esteban Gallardo, a computer engineer with 20 years of experience developing multimedia, game, VR and mobile solutions. From small start-ups to corporations I've contributed with my expertise. Lately, I've explored the potential of AI in audio-book creation and virtual office simulations that test candidates' soft skills.

In the beginning we needed to deploy the J2ME project from lower end devices to high end devices, a solid scalable structure was key in order to deliver to such wide range of platforms.

Flash development opened a world of infinite possibilities allowing individuals like me to create games that reached millions and also helping corporations build browser-based applications for their games and multimedia products.

With Unity the process of creating 3D worlds for mobile eased considerably. This new challenge helped me explore new creative ideas and later assist other developers as a freelance Unity programmer.

From the early VR devices like Oculus Go to the latest generation devices like Meta Quest 3, I've developed immersive game experiences and collaborated with teams to build VR worlds.

The opportunity to share my knowledge with future generations revealed a passion for teaching I wasn't aware of. Now I'm fully committed to designing the best technopedagogical experiences.
