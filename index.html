Story Book Editor: Create a your audiobooks in record time with a fraction of the cost.

In this project I explored a passion of mine that was written sci-fi stories. The tool not only helps in the creative writing process, but it also streamlines the audiobook creation process. You can convert any story into an audiobook, with multiple different voices assigned to the different characters of the story, in record time at a fraction of the cost of the production of a full-fledge audiobook. This solution was meant to help small authors who didn't have resources to create audiobooks for their stories
